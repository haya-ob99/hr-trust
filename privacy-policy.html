Privacy Policy

HR-Trust is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you engage with us, visit our website, or use our services.

Who We Are

HR-Trust is a strategic Human Capital consultancy firm based in Saudi Arabia, offering services in Consulting, Executive Search, Learning & Development, and Executive Coaching.

What Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information (name, email, phone number, job title, company).
  • Professional Data (CVs/resumes, employment history, qualifications, references).
  • Usage Data (how you use our website or services).
  • Communication Records (emails, calls, messages exchanged with us).

For executive search or coaching services, we may collect more detailed professional and behavioral data with your consent.

How We Collect Your Information

We collect your data through:

  • Direct communication with you (email, phone, meetings, online forms).
  • Applications for services or job opportunities.
  • Website usage tracking (via cookies and analytics tools).
  • Referrals or recommendations (e.g., from partners or clients).

How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:
  • Provide and manage our services
  • Match candidates with job opportunities
  • Deliver personalized coaching or training solutions
  • Analyze and improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We only use your information for the purpose it was collected for and retain it only as long as necessary.

Legal Basis for Processing

    We process your data based on:
  • Your consent.
  • The performance of a contract.
  • Our legitimate business interests.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

    We may share your information with:
  • Our consultants and employees involved in delivering services.
  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., IT support, analytics).
  • Client organizations (with your consent for job placement or executive search).

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

Data Security

    We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. These include:
  • Encryption and secure storage.
  • Access controls and authentication.
  • Regular system audits and updates.

International Transfers

    If we transfer your data outside Saudi Arabia, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:
  • Data transfer agreements.
  • Compliance with international privacy standards.

Your Rights

    You have the right to:
  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal limitations).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Object to or restrict data processing.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: info@hr-trust.com

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. We use tools like Google Analytics to understand website traffic and improve our services.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in law or our practices. The updated version will be posted on our website with the new effective date.
